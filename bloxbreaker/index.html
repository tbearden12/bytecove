Frequently Asked Questions

What is Blox Breaker?

Blox Breaker is a neon-fueled block-breaking arcade game for iOS. Smash through 400+ levels across 8 unique worlds, collect power-ups, customize your ball with skins and glow effects, and compete in daily challenges and endless mode.

Is Blox Breaker free to play?

Yes! Blox Breaker is free to download and play. The first 5 worlds (250 levels), endless mode, daily challenges, and the customization shop are all available for free. Premium unlocks additional worlds, exclusive power-ups, and bonus rewards.

Does Blox Breaker require an internet connection?

No! Blox Breaker works completely offline. All game progress and data are stored locally on your device. An internet connection is only needed for purchasing subscriptions or restoring purchases through the App Store.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through Apple. Go to Settings (iPhone) → Your Name → Subscriptions → Blox Breaker → Cancel Subscription. You'll retain Premium access until the end of your current billing period.
