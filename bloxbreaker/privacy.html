Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

Blox Breaker, developed by Bytecove Software ("we," "our," or "us"), is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Information Stored Locally

  • Game Progress: Your level progress, world completion, coins, gems, power-up inventory, daily challenge streaks, and high scores are stored locally on your device.
  • Preferences: Equipped ball skins, glow effects, trail effects, hit effects, and other customization selections are stored locally on your device.
  • Shop Purchases: Records of in-game items you have purchased with coins and gems are stored locally on your device.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Subscription Status: We verify your subscription status through Apple's StoreKit framework to determine Premium access. This is handled entirely by Apple.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any personally identifiable information
  • We do not require account creation or sign-in
  • We do not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs
  • We do not collect device identifiers, IP addresses, or location data
  • We do not share any data with third parties

How We Use Your Information

  • To save your game progress so you can continue where you left off
  • To track your in-game currency (coins and gems) and purchased items
  • To manage your equipped customization items (ball skins, glow effects, trails, etc.)
  • To verify your Premium subscription status through Apple StoreKit
  • To grant Premium benefits such as power-up starter packs, 2x earnings, and access to premium worlds

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Apple StoreKit: We use Apple's StoreKit framework for subscription management and in-app purchase verification. Apple's privacy practices apply to these transactions. Please review Apple's Privacy Policy for more information.

We do not use any other third-party services, SDKs, or frameworks that collect user data.

Data Storage and Security

  • All game data is stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage mechanisms (UserDefaults and local file storage)
  • We do not maintain any servers or databases that store your personal information
  • We do not transmit your game data over the internet
  • Your data remains entirely on your device under your control

Data Retention

  • Your game data persists on your device until you delete the app
  • Deleting the Blox Breaker app will permanently remove all locally stored game data
  • Subscription status is managed by Apple and persists independently of the app

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete all your data at any time by uninstalling the app
  • Manage your subscription through your Apple ID account settings
  • Request information about what data we process (which is none beyond what is stored locally on your device)

Children's Privacy

Blox Breaker does not collect any personal information from any users, including children. The game does not require account creation, does not have social features, and does not collect or transmit personal data. In-app purchases require Apple ID authentication, which is managed by Apple and subject to parental controls.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
