Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play Blox Breaker?

Tap and drag to aim your shot, then release to launch balls at the blocks above. Break all the blocks to complete the level. Collect power-ups that drop from broken blocks to gain temporary abilities. Progress through worlds by completing levels in order.

What are the different power-ups?

There are several power-ups available during gameplay. Free power-ups are available to all players, while Premium subscribers unlock 5 exclusive power-ups: Splash Damage (area damage), Piercing Shots (pass through blocks), Lightning Chain (chain lightning), Mega Bomb (massive explosion), and 2x Pickups (double pickup value).

How do daily challenges work?

A new challenge is available every day. Complete it to earn bonus coins. Build a 7-day streak for increasing rewards, with a special bonus (including gems) on Day 7. If you miss a day, your streak resets. You can check your current streak and the next challenge from the Daily Challenge screen on the main menu.

How do I use the shop?

From the main menu, tap the Shop button. Browse ball skins, glow effects, bullet trails, hit effects, and block break animations. Items are purchased with coins and gems earned through gameplay. Once purchased, equip items from the shop to use them in your games.

What are ball glow effects?

Ball glow effects add a colored glow around your ball during gameplay. There are 15 glow colors to choose from, each costing 1,000 coins. Glow effects are found in the Effects tab of the shop under the "Ball Glow" section. You can disable the glow at any time by equipping "None."

How do I get Premium?

Tap the crown icon or "Go Premium" button from the main menu. Choose between Monthly ($0.99/month) and Yearly ($9.99/year) plans. Premium unlocks 3 additional worlds (150 levels), 5 exclusive power-ups, 2x coin and gem earnings, a free continue per game, and a starter power-up pack.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through Apple. Go to Settings (iPhone) → Your Name → Subscriptions → Blox Breaker → Cancel Subscription. You'll retain Premium access until the end of your current billing period.

How do I restore my purchases?

If you reinstall the app or get a new device, go to the Premium screen and tap "Restore Purchases." This will restore your Premium subscription if you have an active one. In-game currency and shop purchases are stored locally and cannot be restored across devices.

I lost my game progress. Can it be recovered?

Game progress is stored locally on your device. If you delete the app, your progress is permanently lost. We recommend keeping regular device backups through iCloud or iTunes to protect your game data. We are unable to recover lost progress as we do not store any data on external servers.

The app isn't working correctly. What should I do?

Try these steps:

  • Force quit and reopen the app
  • Update to the latest version of Blox Breaker from the App Store
  • Restart your device
  • Make sure your device is running a supported version of iOS
  • Check that your device has enough free storage space

If issues persist, contact us below with details about the problem.

What devices are supported?

Blox Breaker is available on iPhone and iPad running a supported version of iOS/iPadOS. For the best experience, we recommend keeping your device updated to the latest iOS version.
