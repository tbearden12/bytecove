FAQ

Common questions

What is FieldCalc Pro?

A professional trade calculator app with 28 code-compliant calculators for electricians, HVAC technicians, and contractors. References NEC 2023, ASHRAE, IRC 2021, and ACI 318-19.

Does it work without internet?

Yes. Every calculation runs locally on your device. No internet, no account, no data collection. Built for the job site.

Which calculators are free?

6 calculators are free: Ohm's Law, Voltage Drop (Electrical), BTU Heat Load, CFM Airflow (HVAC), Concrete Volume, Paint Coverage (Construction). Pro unlocks all 28.

What code standards are referenced?

NEC 2023 for electrical, ASHRAE 62.1-2022 for HVAC ventilation, ACCA Manual J/S for HVAC loads, IRC 2021 for stairs, and ACI 318-19 for concrete/rebar.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions on your device. You can cancel anytime and retain access until the end of your billing period.
