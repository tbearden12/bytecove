Getting Started

Step 1

Download from the App Store

Search for "FieldCalc Pro" on the App Store or tap the download link on our main page. The app is free to download with optional Pro upgrades.

Step 2

Choose Your Trade

During onboarding, select your primary trade -- Electrical, HVAC, or Construction -- to customize your calculator dashboard.

Step 3

Free vs Pro

Frequently Asked Questions

A 7-day free trial is available during onboarding when you first launch the app, or at any time from Settings > Subscription. You won't be charged until the trial period ends.

On your device, go to Settings > your Apple ID > Subscriptions. Find FieldCalc Pro and tap Cancel Subscription. Your access continues until the end of the current billing period.

In the app, go to Settings > Subscription > Restore Purchases. This will restore your active subscription on the current device.

After completing a calculation, tap the camera button to attach a job-site photo. Photos are stored locally with your calculation data. This is a Pro feature.

iCloud sync is coming soon in a future update. Once available, you'll be able to enable it from Settings to sync your projects and calculations across all your Apple devices.

Go to the Reports tab, select the project you want to export, then tap Export CSV. The file can be shared via email, AirDrop, or saved to Files. This is a Pro feature.

Open any calculator and tap the batch icon in the toolbar to enter batch mode. This lets you run multiple calculations in sequence and save them all to a single project. This is a Pro feature.

Go to Settings > Account > Delete All Data. This will permanently remove all your projects, calculations, photos, and preferences from the app.

No. All calculations and data are stored locally on your device. Nothing is transmitted to any server unless iCloud sync is enabled in a future update, in which case data syncs through Apple's CloudKit.
