Cookie & Tracking Notice

Last updated: May 29, 2026

The short version: we do not use cookies to track you, and we run no analytics or advertising on bytecovesoftware.com. This notice explains the small number of third-party services involved when you load or interact with the site.

Cookies we set

This website does not set any cookies of its own for analytics, advertising, or tracking. It is a set of static pages, and browsing it does not require you to accept cookies. Because we do not place tracking cookies, you will not see a cookie-consent pop-up on our site.

No analytics or advertising

We do not use Google Analytics, Meta (Facebook) Pixel, advertising networks, session-replay tools, or similar tracking technologies. We do not build advertising profiles, and we do not track you across other websites. Because we do not track visitors, there is no behavioral data for us to sell or share, and we honor "Do Not Track" and Global Privacy Control signals simply by not tracking in the first place.

Third-party services

A few third-party services are involved in running the site. Some of them may receive limited technical information, or set their own cookies, only in the situations described below:

  • Google Fonts. We load our typefaces from Google's font servers. When a page loads, your browser requests those fonts, and Google may receive your IP address as part of that request. Google Fonts does not set cookies on your browser.
  • Formspree. When — and only when — you submit our contact form, your submission is sent to Formspree, which delivers it to our inbox and screens it for spam. Formspree may use cookies or similar technology as part of processing that submission and preventing abuse.
  • Calendly. If you click to book a call, you leave our site and go to Calendly, where Calendly's own cookies and privacy practices apply. Calendly is not embedded on our pages, so it is not involved unless you choose to use it.
  • Static web hosting. Our hosting provider serves the site and may keep standard server logs (such as IP address and browser type) for security and reliability. This is server-side logging, not browser cookies.

For more on what we collect and how we use it, see our Privacy Policy.

Managing cookies in your browser

You are always in control of cookies through your browser settings, where you can view, block, or delete cookies for any site. Because we do not set tracking cookies, blocking cookies will not affect your ability to browse this website. If you visit a third-party service linked from our site, that service's own controls and notices apply.

Changes to this notice

If we ever add a service that uses cookies or tracking, we will update this notice — and, where the law requires it, ask for your consent first. When we make changes, we will revise the "Last updated" date above.

Contact us

Questions about cookies or tracking on this site? Email support@bytecovesoftware.com or tbearden@bytecovesoftware.com.
