FAQ

Common questions

What is WOMBAT?

WOMBAT (Work Order Management & Business Administration Tool) is a multi-tenant SaaS platform for project-driven service teams. It centralizes work order tracking, project management with Gantt charts, time and labor entry, and operational analytics in one system.

Who is WOMBAT designed for?

Systems integrators, contractors, technical service teams, IT service providers, maintenance groups, and any team that manages work orders and needs visibility into labor and project performance.

How does multi-tenancy work?

Each organization gets its own fully isolated environment with a unique organization code. Data is completely separated between tenants. Users log in with their organization code, username, and password.

Does WOMBAT replace accounting or ERP software?

No. WOMBAT focuses on operational visibility — work tracking, labor management, job costing, and project performance. It complements your accounting system by giving you real-time profitability data per job, without the overhead of ERP.

How does job costing work?

Set bill rates per task or work order, and cost rates per employee. WOMBAT automatically calculates revenue, labor cost, profit, and margin as time entries are logged. Financial data is only visible to users with admin or accounting roles — field workers never see rates or margins.

Can I try it before committing?

Yes. We offer guided demos where we walk you through the platform with your own data and workflows. Book a demo through our contact page.
