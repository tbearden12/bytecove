Frequently Asked Questions

What is WordBrew?

WordBrew is a cozy tea-themed word puzzle game for iOS. Connect letters on a circular wheel to form words and fill crossword grids. With 250 levels across 10 chapters, a deep cosmetic shop, collectible tea cups, and ambient sound packs, it's designed to be the perfect relaxation companion.

Is WordBrew free to play?

Yes! The first 5 chapters (125 levels), daily brew challenges, the cosmetic shop, and all customization features are completely free. Premium chapters 6–10 can be unlocked for $1.99 each or $4.99 for all five. You can also remove ads for $2.99.

What are tea leaves and how do I earn them?

Tea leaves are WordBrew's in-game currency. Earn them by completing levels, finding bonus words, claiming daily login rewards, and watching rewarded ads. Equipping a tea cup from the collection also gives you a percentage bonus on all earned tea leaves (5%–30% depending on the cup).

Can I sync my progress across devices?

Yes! Go to Settings and sign in with Apple. Your stars, tea leaves, unlocked cosmetics, and level completion data sync automatically to iCloud. The sync always favors the player — you never lose progress when merging between devices.

Does WordBrew require an internet connection?

No! WordBrew works completely offline. An internet connection is only needed for purchasing items through the App Store, syncing progress via iCloud, and loading ads (which can be removed with a one-time purchase).
