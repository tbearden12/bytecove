1. Description of Service

WordBrew is a word puzzle game that allows you to:

  • Play through 250 levels across 10 themed chapters
  • Form words by connecting letters on a circular wheel
  • Complete crossword grids to earn stars and tea leaves
  • Customize your experience with tile themes, trail effects, wheel skins, completion animations, tea cups, font styles, and sound packs
  • Use power-ups (Auto Solve, Time Freeze, Letter Reveal) to assist gameplay
  • Complete daily brew challenges for bonus rewards
  • Sign in with Apple to sync progress across devices

Piggy Bank

  • The Piggy Bank automatically accumulates 10% of all tea leaves you earn during gameplay

  • After breaking, the Piggy Bank resets and begins accumulating again
