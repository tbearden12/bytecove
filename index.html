01 The Problem

Important work, managed in disconnected tools.

Spreadsheets, email threads, group texts, and a folder of PDFs nobody has opened in six months. Most small-to-midsize operations aren't short on data — they're short on anywhere it actually lives.

  • Work tracked in multiple places. Three spreadsheets, one CRM, and someone's memory.
  • Data entered twice — or three times. Paper ticket to whiteboard to QuickBooks.
  • No clear view of progress or labor. By the time you see a problem, it's already billed.
  • Reports that take half a day. Export, paste, fix the numbers, email it out.
  • Systems that don't match how your team actually works. You adapt to the software, not the other way around.

09 Frequently Asked

Straight answers, no pitch deck.

What does Bytecove Software do?

We design and deploy purpose-built internal systems, AI integrations, analytics dashboards, and workflow automation for service-based organizations. We fit in the gap between spreadsheets and enterprise systems — configured to how your team actually works.

What industries does Bytecove serve?

Engineering and technical service firms, contractors and specialty trades, IT and managed service providers, operations and maintenance teams, and professional service firms. If your team manages work by job number, service request, or project, we're a fit.

How is Bytecove different from off-the-shelf software?

Generic SaaS asks you to adapt to its model. We build lightweight, purpose-built systems configured to your terminology, your workflow, and your team. Each system is deployed privately for your organization and designed for long-term use. We're not an ERP replacement — we fit in the gap between spreadsheets and enterprise systems.

Where is Bytecove based?

Chattanooga, Tennessee. We work with clients across the Southeast and remotely across the US, but we prioritize engagements within about an hour of Chattanooga so we can meet in person when it matters.

How much does a project cost?

Projects typically start at $2,500. Final pricing depends on scope and complexity, which we work through together during the initial workflow review. Most projects for small to mid-size teams fall in the $5,000–$25,000 range. We quote fixed prices tied to specific outcomes, not hourly retainers.

How do I get started with Bytecove?

Schedule a free 15-minute workflow review. We discuss how you currently track work, identify areas for improvement, and outline what a purpose-built system could look like for your team. No commitment required.
