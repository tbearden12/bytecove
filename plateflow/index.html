Frequently Asked Questions

What is PlateFlow?

PlateFlow is an AI-powered meal planning app that creates personalized meal plans tailored to your diet goals, allergies, and preferences. It includes smart grocery lists, nutrition tracking, barcode scanning, and recipe management.

What diet types does PlateFlow support?

PlateFlow supports over 8 diet types including Keto, Vegan, Mediterranean, Paleo, Weight Loss, Muscle Building, Diabetic-friendly, and more. Each meal plan is tailored to your specific dietary requirements.

Does PlateFlow require an internet connection?

PlateFlow requires an internet connection for AI meal plan generation. However, your saved meal plans, grocery lists, and nutrition data sync via iCloud and are available across all your devices.

How does AI meal planning work in PlateFlow?

PlateFlow uses AI to generate personalized weekly meal plans based on your diet type, calorie goals, allergies, and food preferences. The AI creates balanced meals with recipes, automatically generates organized grocery lists, and tracks your nutrition intake.
